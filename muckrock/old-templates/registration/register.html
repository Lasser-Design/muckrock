Making Access Easy

For the past two years, MuckRock has helped journalists, researchers and everyday citizens file Freedom of Information Requests, breaking national news and illuminating local issues. We're now pleased to open up our site for a public beta: Things are occasionally rough around the edges, but we stand behind all of our paid products with a 30-day 100% money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you want your questions answered, MuckRock can help by merging the best of web technology with an active, enthusiastic community of document hounds. For organizational accounts or any other questions, please e-mail Michael Morisy at Michael@MuckRock.com.
