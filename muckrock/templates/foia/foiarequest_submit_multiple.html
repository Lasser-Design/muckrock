Submit a Request to Multiple Agencies

Selecting an agency type will restrict the agencies to those of that type. Leave it blank for all types. Selecting a jurisdiction will restrict it to agencies directly in that jurisdiction or one level below. Leave it blank to select agencies from all jurisdictions. After submitting, you will be shown a list of all agencies that matched your criteria and asked to confirm that you want to submit this request to all of them.
